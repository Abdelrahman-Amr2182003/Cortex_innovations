Lab Facilities
Thermal camera

Cortex Innovations is equipped with state-of-the-art thermal cameras, enabling precise temperature measurements and thermal imaging. Our thermal camera technology plays a critical role in various applications, including research, quality control, and product development.

Salinity meters

Our advanced salinity meters provide accurate measurements of salt concentration in liquids. Whether analyzing water quality or conducting experiments in marine environments, Cortex Innovations' salinity meters ensure reliable and precise results.

Torque meter

Cortex Innovations employs torque meters for precise measurement of rotational forces. This technology is essential for assessing the performance and efficiency of mechanical systems, ensuring optimal functionality and reliability.

PLC systems

Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) systems at Cortex Innovations enable automation and control in various industrial processes. Our PLC systems are tailored to specific applications, enhancing efficiency, reliability, and adaptability in diverse operations.

Lidar sensors

Lidar sensors are a cornerstone of our sensor technology arsenal, providing precise distance and mapping data. Cortex Innovations leverages lidar sensors for applications ranging from environmental monitoring to autonomous navigation.

Taco meters

Tachometers are vital instruments for measuring rotational speed. Cortex Innovations utilizes tachometers in various applications, ensuring accurate speed control and monitoring in mechanical systems.

Oscilloscope

Our cutting-edge oscilloscopes enable detailed visualization and analysis of electrical signals. Cortex Innovations employs oscilloscopes for diagnostics, troubleshooting, and optimizing electronic circuits and systems.

3D Printing

Cortex Innovations' 3D printing facility revolutionizes prototyping and small-scale production. Our high-precision 3D printers bring designs to life, fostering innovation in product development and manufacturing.

Laser engraver

Cortex Innovations employs laser engraving technology for intricate and precise markings on various materials. From branding to customization, our laser engraver adds a layer of sophistication to diverse applications.
